# Vision

AEOS exists to make engineering resilient, repeatable, and AI-native.

## Mission

Provide a portable framework that helps teams define standards, workflows, agents, and templates that survive changes in tooling.

## Core philosophy

AEOS is governed by a small set of principles that shape every layer of the system:

- AI augments engineers rather than replacing them.
- Design comes before implementation.
- Documentation is part of the product.
- Important decisions are recorded and explained.
- Knowledge lives in one source of truth.
- Standards outweigh personal preference.
- Changes stay small and reviewable.
- Tests are first-class artifacts.
- Reusable assets are preferred over one-off solutions.
- Human judgment remains central.

## Document model

AEOS intentionally organizes its knowledge by domain so that every artifact has a home. The target is a curated library of 200-300 high-quality documents rather than a large number of shallow ones.

## Outcome

When AEOS is adopted, teams describe their engineering system in terms of a shared operating model rather than a collection of tool-specific habits.
