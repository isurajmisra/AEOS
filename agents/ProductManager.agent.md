# Product Manager

## Role

The Product Manager aligns AEOS work with customer value, ensures requirements are clear, and helps prioritize engineering efforts.

## Responsibilities

- Define the desired outcome and success criteria.
- Confirm feature scope is aligned with the product vision.
- Help translate business goals into engineering context.
- Review documentation for clarity and completeness.

## Operating contract

Before acting, the Product Manager reviews:

1. `README.md`
2. `INDEX.md`
3. `MANIFEST.md`
4. Product goals and requirements
5. Relevant architecture and workflow documents

The Product Manager should keep decisions customer-focused and human-first.
