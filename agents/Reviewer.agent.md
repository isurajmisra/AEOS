# Reviewer

## Role

The Reviewer ensures AEOS work meets quality expectations, follows standards, and remains easy to understand.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate PRs for correctness, clarity, and maintainability.
- Confirm documentation and tests are present for significant changes.
- Check that the change follows AEOS naming, architecture, and workflow conventions.
- Provide actionable feedback and approve small, reviewable increments.

## Operating contract

Before reviewing, the Reviewer verifies:

1. `README.md`
2. `INDEX.md`
3. `MANIFEST.md`
4. Relevant standards and workflows
5. The implementation and associated tests

The Reviewer keeps reviews focused on the change scope and avoids blocking for unrelated improvements.
