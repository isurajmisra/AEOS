# Naming Standard

## Purpose

Clear and consistent naming makes AEOS artifacts easier to discover, understand, and use.

## Rules

- Use descriptive, predictable names.
- Follow the AEOS suffix convention:
  - `*.agent.md`
  - `*.skill.md`
  - `*.standard.md`
  - `*.workflow.md`
- Keep names short but meaningful.
- Prefer nouns for artifacts and roles.

## Review

- Confirm that new files use the correct suffix.
- Ensure names match the domain and purpose of the document.
- Avoid ambiguous or overly generic labels.
